How to play

The original Simon game was created by Ralph H. Baer and Howard J. Morrison, working for toy design firm Marvin Glass and Associates.

To play the chicken version of the game, You must first click the start button and wait for one of the chickens to light up at random.

After the computer is finished, it's up to you to copy the sequence. Should you succeed, the series will become progressivley longer.

Should you fail, the game is over and will restart. You need to pass ten rounds in order to win. have fun!
